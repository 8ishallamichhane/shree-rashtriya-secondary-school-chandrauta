Admission Process

Step 1: Application

Submit your application form along with required documents

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate
  • Previous academic records
  • Passport size photographs

Step 2: Entrance Test

Take the entrance examination to evaluate academic readiness

  • Mathematics
  • English
  • General Knowledge

Step 3: Interview

Personal interview with student and parents

Step 4: Admission

Final selection and fee payment
