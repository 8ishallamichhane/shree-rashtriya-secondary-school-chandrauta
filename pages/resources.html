Frequently Asked Questions

What are the school hours?

School hours for Day Shift(Grade 11-12 and Diploma) are from 6:00 AM to 11:00 PM and for Day Shift(Pre-Primary - Grade 10) are from 10:30 AM to 4:30 PM, Sunday through Friday. The school office is open from 10:00 AM to 4:30 PM.

How do I report my child's absence?

Please call the Class Teacher or the Class Coordinator before class starts on the day of absence.

What is the dress code policy?

Details about our dress code can be found in the school handbook. Students are expected to dress appropriately for a learning environment.

How can I contact my child's teachers?

You can contact teachers through the school office or via their school email addresses listed in the staff directory.
