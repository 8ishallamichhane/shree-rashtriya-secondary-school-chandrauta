Our Academic Programs

Playgroup

For playgroup or preschool classes, the focus is typically on early childhood development through play-based learning. The subjects are designed to be interactive and engaging, fostering social, emotional, and cognitive growth.

  • Alphabets and Numbers
  • Shapes and Colors
  • Art and Craft
  • Speaking and Listening
  • Sorting and Grouping
  • Coloring and Drawing

Primary Level (Grade 1-5)

The primary level focuses on building essential skills in students through a balanced curriculum that includes academic subjects, physical activities, arts, and social development. Students in Grades 1-5 begin to form a deeper understanding of the world around them, while also developing critical thinking, creativity, and social skills. The goal is to lay a strong foundation for lifelong learning and well-rounded personal development

  • English
  • Nepali
  • Social Studies

Lower Secondary Level (Grade 6-8)

Our lower secondary level program provides a balanced curriculum that includes academic subjects, physical activities, arts, and social development. Students in Grades 6-8 begin to form a deeper understanding of the world around them, while also developing critical thinking, creativity, and social skills. The goal is to lay a strong foundation for lifelong learning and well-rounded personal development

  • English
  • Nepali

Secondary Level (Grade 9-12)

Specialized streams for grades 9-12 preparing students for higher education.

  • English

Diploma
